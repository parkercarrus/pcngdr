Generative AI Model for Optimal Tree Placement

Independently developed and produced a generative-AI model for predicting optimal tree placement in urban areas. Allows users to input their location and calculates the optimal locations for tree planting near them. Has applications in urban planning and sustainable development.

Urban canopy areas are identified and segmented. This helps urban planners to understand the current distribution of tree cover and plan for future plantings. The model uses advanced image processing techniques to accurately identify these areas, providing valuable data for sustainable development projects.

The model also calculates the potential benefits of planting trees in different areas, such as reducing urban heat and improving air quality. This information is crucial for making informed decisions about where to invest in green infrastructure. By focusing on areas with the highest potential benefits, cities can maximize the positive impact of their tree planting efforts.

Overall, this generative-AI model is a powerful tool for urban planners and environmentalists. It provides detailed insights into the current state of urban forests and helps to identify the best locations for new plantings. With its ability to analyze large amounts of data quickly and accurately, it can significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of urban tree planting initiatives.

Satellite imagery is gathered based on inputted location.

Urban canopy areas are identified and segmented.

Areas with the highest potential to mitigate urban heat through tree planting are highlighted in green.
